Kobe x Chadwick Memorial

This illustrated image is a memorial for the great Kobe Bryant & Chadwick Boseman who recently passed away this year. They have shown me that anything is possible as long as you put in the effort & be patient because it's not going to happen over night.

For Kobe Bryants illustration I used Adobe Illustrator using the pen tool & for Chadwick Boseman I used Procreate to test my skills & to try it out.

The background image is a purple galaxy to resemble their signature colors & to tell us that wherever they are now, even if they are in a new life or a galaxy far far away. They will always be here with us in spirit because they both have impacted many lives not only as celebrities but also as people who try their best to inspire in order to strive forward for greatness.
